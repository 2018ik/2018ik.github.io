## Background

The OT reveals five basic levitical **offerings**:

1. Burnt offering
2. Meal offering
3. Peace offering
4. Sin offering
5. Trespass offering

There are further offerings such as:

1. Heave offering
2. Wave offering
3. Drink offering
4. Firstfruits
5. Voluntary offerings
