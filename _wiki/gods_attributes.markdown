## Background

God has many **attributes** revealed in the Bible. This includes:

1. Trinity
   1. God being three and one is the highest attribute of God. This is what He is at the core of His being.
2. Peace
3. Love
4. Grace
5. Mercy
6. Kindness
7. Joy
8. Hope
9. Glory
