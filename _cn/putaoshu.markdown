## Background

**葡萄树** means grapevine, though it is also the translation for "vine" in the Bible as it is implied to be a grape vine.

## Example Usage

>我是真葡萄树，我辐射栽培的人（约十五1）。

Tranlation: I am the true vine, and My Father is the husbandman.
