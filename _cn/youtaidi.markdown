**犹太地** is Judea.

1. 犹太 (Yóutài): This means "Jewish" or "Judaism." It refers to the Jewish people or the Jewish religion.
2. 地 (dì): This means "land" or "territory."

So, "犹太地" literally translates to "Jewish land" or Judea.
