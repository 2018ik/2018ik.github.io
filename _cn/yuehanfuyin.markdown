## Background

**约翰福音** is the Gospel of John.

## Example Usage

**约翰福音**主要的是记载主在犹太地的行动，以及祂在那里所说深奥的话。

The Gospel of John mainly records the Lord's move in Judea and the profound words He spoke there.
